To My Love

I'm truly sorry for hurting you. You never deserved the pain my actions and words caused. The last thing I ever want is to be the reason behind your tears.


I know an apology doesn't magically fix things, and I know trust takes time. But I need you to know I genuinely regret everything I did. Not because of how it made things but because I care about you, deeply, and I hate that I made you feel otherwise.


Thank you for loving me. For being there through so many moments. For making my life so much brighter in ways I can't even put into words. I don't take your love for granted even when it might've seemed like I did.


I promise to be more understanding, more patient, and better for you and for us. You deserve kindness, effort, and love every single day.


No matter what, I'll always cherish every laugh, every photo, every little adventure we've shared together.


I miss your smile. I miss us. And I really hope we can make this right.

I'm sorry, baby. Lots of love, vihu ❤️
